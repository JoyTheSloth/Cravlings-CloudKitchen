CRAVLINGS

Build Your Cravling. Feed Your Craving.

Project Type: Frontend UI/UX Case Study (No Backend)

Concept: Cravlings is a mood-first food ordering experience where users
discover meals based on what they’re craving instead of browsing endless
restaurant lists. Every user has a virtual companion called a “Cravling”
that evolves with their food preferences.

Core Features - Mood-based food discovery - AI-inspired “Feed Your
Cravling” flow - Personalized recommendations - Rewards & XP system -
Cravling evolution - Clean, playful mascot-driven branding - Modern
cloud kitchen inspired UI

Brand Personality - Fun - Warm - Friendly - Premium - Playful - Gamified

Primary Colors - Warm Yellow - Coral Red - Soft Orange - Cream White -
Charcoal Black

Bottom Navigation 1. Home 2. Search 3. Feed Me (Primary CTA) 4. Orders
5. Profile
